Snooze Tabs

Save tabs for later in one click. Choose "Later Today", "Tomorrow", or pick a custom date.

Auto Restore

Tabs automatically reopen when the time comes. No annoying notifications, just focus.

Keyboard First

Blazing fast shortcuts. Use Cmd + . to open, single letters to snooze. Fully customizable.

How to use

  1. Quick Command

    Press Cmd + . to open. Customizable in Chrome settings.

  2. Instant Snooze

    Type L for Later, T for Tomorrow, or P to Pick Date. Customize keys and themes in Options.

  3. Focus & Restore

    Enjoy your clean workspace. Tabs magically reappear exactly when you need them.
