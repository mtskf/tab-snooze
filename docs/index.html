Snooze Tabs

Save tabs for later in one click. Choose "Later Today", "Tomorrow", or pick a custom date.

Auto Restore

Tabs automatically reopen when the time comes. No annoying notifications, just focus.

Keyboard First

Blazing fast shortcuts. Use Cmd + . to open, numbers to snooze. Never lift your hands.

How to use

  1. Quick Command

    Press Cmd + . to open. No mouse needed.

  2. Instant Snooze

    Type 1 for later, 2 for this evening, or P to pick a date. It's that simple.

  3. Focus & Restore

    Enjoy your clean workspace. Tabs magically reappear exactly when you need them.
