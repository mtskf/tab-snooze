focus on now. Snooze for later.
Declutter your browser and your mind with Snooooze, the privacy-focused tab manager designed for productivity pros.

## Why Snooooze?

Too many open tabs? Afraid to close them because you might forget? Snooooze lets you temporarily stash tabs away until you're actually ready to deal with them. They disappear now and magically reopen exactly when you need them.

## Shortcuts (Customizable)

*   `Cmd/Ctrl` + `.`: Open Snooooze Popup
*   `L`: Snooze for Later Today
*   `E`: Snooze for This Evening
*   `T`: Snooze for Tomorrow
*   `S`: Snooze for This Weekend
*   `N`: Snooze for Next Monday
*   `P`: Pick a Custom Date

> **Tip:** Hold `Shift` while pressing any shortcut to snooze your **entire window** at once!
